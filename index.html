Questions
Frequently asked.
What level do I need to join?
None at all — we welcome complete beginners right through to advanced speakers looking to reach near-native fluency. Just select your current level when you sign up.
Which platforms do you teach on?
We teach on Zoom, Google Meet, and Microsoft Teams. Just pick the one you already use — no new accounts or downloads needed on your end.
How long are the classes?
Standard sessions are 60 minutes for 1-on-1 classes and 90 minutes for group sessions. We also offer 30-minute intensive mini-sessions for busy schedules — just ask when we reach out.
Is there a free trial?
Yes! After you sign up, we'll invite you to a free 15-minute intro call so we can understand your goals and make sure we're the right fit before you commit to anything.
What types of English do you teach?
Conversational English, Business English, Academic English, exam preparation (IELTS, TOEFL, Cambridge), and general fluency. Tell us your goal and we'll design a lesson plan around it.
